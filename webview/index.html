Welcome to Dyslexia VS

Easily activate the Dyslexia VS extension with a simple button from your keyboard or click within VS, enabling all reading accessibility features instantly.

Overview

Dyslexia VS is designed to make reading code and web content more accessible for individuals with dyslexia. The extension offers a range of customizable features to cater to various needs, such as dyslexia-friendly fonts, custom background colors, and adjustable text spacing to enhance letter and word distinction. It also includes a text-to-speech function, allowing users to listen to the content for better comprehension. Additionally, the extension features dynamic word highlighting, which helps readers track text as they read. These tools work together to create a personalized reading experience that promotes clarity, ease of reading, and overall accessibility, ensuring a more inclusive and user-friendly digital environment.

Accessibility and Compliance

Dyslexia VS is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for individuals with dyslexia and other reading difficulties. We continuously work towards improving the user experience for everyone, adhering to relevant accessibility standards. Our extension aims to comply with WCAG 2.1 level AA standards, making the web more accessible by offering features that address dyslexia and reading challenges.
